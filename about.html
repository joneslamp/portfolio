About Jones Lampa

Dr Jones Lampa Turchet graduated from the University of Toronto in 1978. Upon graduation, he completed a residency program at Mount Sinai Hospital in Toronto. On a humanitarian trip to Haiti, he also provided dentistry to the underserved and subsequently raised funds and equipment for other trips. He did the same for residents of the Attawapiskat First Nation, located on James Bay in Northern Ontario.

He practices at more than 10 locations in the Chicago area, specializing in periodontics and implants. Dr. Jones Lampa teaches implant and periodontology in Russia, Ukraine, the Baltics, India, and several other European countries for the Chicago Center for Advanced Dentistry and the University of Chicago. In his spare time, he enjoys traveling, scuba diving, cooking, running, reading, and spending as much time as possible with his daughter, Ali. Dr. Nick has run all five major world marathons and is working on completing a marathon in all 50 states.

Dr Jones Lampa is a highly skilled dental implant specialist. With decades of experience and thousands of implants placed, Dr Jones Lampa also provides dental implant training to his colleagues and is the leading Ceramic Implant Specialist on implant . He offers ceramic implants to patients who do not want traditional titanium implants due to health, cosmetic, or other concerns. Ceramic implants are made from zirconia and are white in color.
