Main Services

With a genuine love for dentistry and optimal oral healthcare, We're dedicated into improving the general aesthetic and function of your teeth so you can easily keep you smile for a lifetime

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is generally used to refer to any dental work that improves the appearance of teeth. It primarily focuses on improvement in dental aesthetics in color, position, shape, size, alignment and overall smile appearance.

Pediatric Dentistry

A pediatric dentist, defined, is a specially trained dentist who focuses on caring for children from infancy through the teenage years, and also adults with special needs.

Dental Implants

A dental implant is a metal post that replaces the root portion of a missing tooth. An artificial tooth (crown) is placed on an extension of the post (abutment) on the dental implant, giving you the look of a real tooth.

Periodontal Therapy

periodontal therapy is an umbrella term for an array of treatments you may need to undergo. These treatments are an important part of restoring health to your teeth and gums, as well as preventing tooth loss.

JDIC

Jones Dental Implant Centre, JDIC have highly skilled dental implant specialists. With decades of experience and thousands of implants placed, JDIC also provides dental implant training to his colleagues and is the leading Ceramic Implant Specialist on implant.
